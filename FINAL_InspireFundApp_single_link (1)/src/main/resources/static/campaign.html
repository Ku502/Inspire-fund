Riya's story

Riya is a bright seven-year-old from Raipur who loves drawing and wants to be a teacher. Last month, she was diagnosed with a congenital heart defect that requires immediate surgical intervention. Without the operation, doctors say her condition will deteriorate rapidly.

The surgery costs ₹1,20,000 — far beyond what her parents, daily-wage workers, can afford. The hospital has given a deadline of 30 days.

Her father Rajesh works as a construction laborer earning ₹350/day. Her mother Sunita is a homemaker caring for Riya and her younger brother. They have exhausted their savings and sold what little gold they had.

Your donation goes directly to Narayana Hospital's patient trust fund, which will be released to cover surgery and post-operative care. Any surplus will be returned to donors proportionally.
